Why Sponsor Us?

As an undergraduate student team, UWNRG competes on a global scale by participating in the International Conference for Robotics and Automation hosted by IEEE and NIST. This means that we are able to offer our sponsors unparalleled exposure and reach to some of the top institutions and companies across the world. During our public appearances, we thank our sponsors in a variety of ways. Our media includes logos on our team shirts as well as recognition in presentations, posters, and promotional events. Your support is crucial to our success, and we strive to maintain a long and mutually beneficial relationship.
